Sushi

Description

This sushi recipe features seasoned rice, fresh fish or veggies, and crispy nori, rolled to perfection. Customize with your favorite ingredients and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for an authentic taste!

Making sushi at home is a fun and rewarding experience that lets you experiment with different flavors and textures. Whether you prefer classic rolls like California or spicy tuna, or want to create your own combinations, this recipe allows for endless creativity. With just a few simple ingredients, you can enjoy fresh, homemade sushi anytime!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar, ½ tsp salt
  • 5 nori sheets
  • 200g fish (salmon, tuna) or crab/shrimp
  • 1 cucumber, 1 avocado (sliced)
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, pickled ginger
  • Bamboo sushi mat

    • Steps

    1. Rinse sushi rice, cook with water, then mix with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let it cool.
    2. Slice fish, cucumber, and avocado into thin strips.
    3. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, shiny side down.
    4. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a small border.
    5. Arrange fish and veggies in a line near the edge of the rice.
    6. Use the bamboo mat to roll tightly, applying gentle pressure.
    7. Cut into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
