Cookies

Description

These homemade cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with rich flavor. Made with simple ingredients like butter, sugar, and chocolate chips, they’re perfect for a sweet treat. Enjoy them fresh out of the oven with a glass of milk!

These cookies are crispy on the edges, soft in the center, and loaded with melty chocolate in every bite. Whether you enjoy them warm from the oven or save them for later, they make the perfect treat for any occasion. Customize them with nuts, caramel, or your favorite mix-ins for even more flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Set Oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let cookies cool slightly before serving!
